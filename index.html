Basic info

BMW, which stands for Bayerische Motoren Werke AG (Bavarian Motor Works), is a German company known for manufacturing luxury vehicles and motorcycles. It is a multinational conglomerate that also offers financial and mobility services. The brand is associated with driver enjoyment and offers a wide range of models, including sedans, SUVs, electric cars (i brand), and sports cars (M models).

Brand features

  • Company structure: The BMW Group includes brands like BMW, MINI, Rolls-Royce, and BMW Motorrad (motorcycles).
  • Product range: The company produces a variety of vehicles, from the standard Series sedans and coupes to X Series SUVs, and a dedicated line of electric cars under the "i" brand. There are also high-performance M models and a line of sports cars like the Z models.
  • Global presence: The BMW Group operates over 30 production sites around the world and has a global sales network in more than 140 countries.
  • Innovation: BMW is also involved in automotive innovation, focusing on vehicle connectivity and the development of future autonomous cars. The company also has a history of creating one-of-a-kind BMW Art Cars with famous artists.
